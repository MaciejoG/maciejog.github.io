First day skiing

Cassy took to the slopes with glee
She was excited to learn how to ski
But as she glided down the mountain side
She couldn't help but glide and slide

She tumbled and she fell
But Maciej was there, he rang the bell
He rescued her and set her straight
And off she went, it wasn't too late

She laughed and she played
As she worked on the tricks she still needed to grade
With Maciej by her side
She no longer feared the snowy slide

As the ski day came to a close
Cassy and Maciej headed to apres-ski, as they chose.
They shared a romantic dinner and a sauna,
Their bond only grew stronger, without any drama.

Now Cassy skis with grace and poise
She's conquered her fears and found her joys
On the mountain, she is free
And skiing became a part of her identity
